Career Counselling

As part of Career Counselling, we help individuals with their education and job aspirations. Based on the current state of individuals, we help them progress towards your aspirations. After understanding one’s achievements and aspirations we provide them with the options and help define a realistic probability of achieving those options. We then move to the next stage of creating a detailed plan (with objectives and timelines) to achieve the option they see best for themselves. The charted plan helps individuals keep track of their progress towards their goals.

College Applications

College Applications are complex. It is not only important to have a great profile but also to be able to convey your qualities and achievements in a way that helps you secure that one seat in the admissions. It is very important for individuals to ensure that right message is being sent across to the admission committee. College applications normally comprises of a written exam, essay writing, GD and PI, the format varies from college to college. We help you with the entire process of college application, starting with written exam, essay writing, resume, SOP, GD and PI’s.
